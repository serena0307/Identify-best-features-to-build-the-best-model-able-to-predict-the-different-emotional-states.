# Identify-best-features-to-build-the-best-model-able-to-predict-the-different-emotional-states.
With advancements in technology and data analytics, the development of an  effective health monitoring system has become a key focus in the field of healthcare.  This project aims to contribute to the enhancement of health monitoring through the  utilization of classification algorithms and ensemble learning techniques. 
The dataset(Dataset_Study2.csv) used in this study contains various health-related features, and the task 
is to predict health conditions based on these features.  The need for accurate 
predictive models arises from the increasing complexity of health data and the desire 
to move towards personalized medicine. As individuals generate vast amounts of 
health-related data through wearables, sensors, and other monitoring devices, the 
challenge lies in extracting meaningful insights from this data to inform healthcare 
decisions. I tried the integration of classification algorithms and ensemble learning 
techniques enhance the accuracy and efficiency of a health monitoring system aim to 
investigate the efficacy of combining multiple models to create an ensemble that 
outperforms individual models in predicting health outcomes.
